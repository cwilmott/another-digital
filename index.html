22-23 March 2026 script.Destroy() CFP →

script.Destroy() invites attendees to help lay the ground work for "another" digital geographies conference, one that centers the work of early-career scholars and practitioners, theorists and activists, thinkers and creators. It emphasizes the digital as a site of critical inquiry and creative practice, rather than a tool for reproducing existing hierarchies and power structures. script.Destroy() calls into question not just how digital geographies are done, but who gets to do them. Join us for an open conversation about building new structures of collaboration, dialogue and critique in preparation for AAG NYC 2027.

AAG NYC
13-15 Feb 2027 Digital Geographies, Otherwise? CFP →

Digital Geographies, Otherwise? is a three-day conference that will take place in New York City on February 3-5, 2027. It will feature a mix of keynote speakers, panel discussions, workshops, and interactive sessions that explore the theme of "Another Digital Is Possible." The conference will bring together scholars, practitioners, and activists from a variety of disciplines to discuss the critical role of digital technologies in creating, perpetuating and resisting oppressive and unequal power structures. The conference will also provide opportunities for building solidarity and community among attendees.
